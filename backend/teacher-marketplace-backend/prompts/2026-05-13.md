Teacher Availibility.
I want to change the interface a teacher has for selecting their availabiliy.
1. platonic availability grid - new screen thats really just a template. It has 4 generic weeks m-s, month view. You click on a particlar day and you zoom into that particular day. all 24 hours are shown as selectable. The teacher then selects as normal their availability.
2. teacher then can press a button to "stamp" there actual month, it then tries to map platonic grid with actual month. the actual month is still source of truth.
their are still things i wish to retain eg. at moment you cant double book a teacher, for example student a books a slot then when student b goes to try and book it it just is marked as unavailable, but the student is not told why, its none of their business.  i think the say can be said of students, they cant double book themselves with different teachers, the system knows the are not available

3. Stamping rules - bookings have priority over the platonic grid.
When a teacher stamps their PAG onto the real calendar, any slot that already has a PENDING or CONFIRMED booking is left completely untouched - the stamp skips it. The teacher cannot unbook a lesson by changing their PAG. Example: teacher has a confirmed Monday 9am lesson, then goes into PAG and removes Monday 9am. When they stamp, that Monday 9am booking is preserved exactly as it was.

4. Conflict indicator on real calendar.
After a stamp (or at any time), if a slot on the real calendar has a booking (PENDING or CONFIRMED) but the current PAG says that slot should be OFF, show a visual conflict indicator on that slot chip - e.g. a coloured border or badge. This tells the teacher "you have a lesson here but your template says you don't want to work then." It is informational only - the booking is still honoured.
